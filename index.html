Sash's 5-Day Lower Body Workout Plan

Day 1: Lower Body & Shoulders

Warm-up

5-10 minutes of brisk walking or cycling

Squats

3 sets of 10-12 reps (bodyweight or light dumbbells to start)

Lunges

3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg

Day 2: Lower Body & Back

Warm-up

5-10 minutes of brisk walking or cycling

Deadlifts

3 sets of 10 reps (light dumbbells or barbell)

Step-ups

3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Day 3: Active Recovery

Walking

30 minutes of brisk walking

Stretching

10-15 minutes of full-body stretching

Day 4: Lower Body & Shoulders

Warm-up

5-10 minutes of brisk walking or cycling

Goblet Squats

3 sets of 10 reps

Reverse Lunges

3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Day 5: Lower Body & Back

Warm-up

5-10 minutes of brisk walking or cycling

Romanian Deadlifts

3 sets of 10 reps (light dumbbells)

Bulgarian Split Squats

3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
